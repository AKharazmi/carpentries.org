Our Instructor Community

This page lists all the instructors who have consented to be listed publicly on our web site. If you are a badged Instructor whose details do not appear here, but you would like to be listed, please update your Instructor profile and agree to make your profile public via the checkbox option within the record. You will need login with your GitHub username to populate this page with your record. If we don't have your GitHub username in your instructor record, please [email it to the team](mailto:team@carpentries.org). Your Twitter, GitHub, ORCiD, and personal website URL as they are provided in your AMY record will appear as part of your record here.
